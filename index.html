I'm Blen Siedeger

Welcome to my portfolio website. I am a professional street art photographer based in Bogota, Colombia. I travel the world to capture vibrant and dynamic street art scenes that inspire me.

My style is characterized by bold colors, strong contrasts, and creative compositions that showcase the details and textures of the artworks. I aim to document and celebrate street art as a form of visual poetry, and my goal is to showcase the beauty and diversity of this art form through my photographs. I hope that my portfolio inspires you to see the world around you with new eyes and appreciate the richness and complexity of street art.
